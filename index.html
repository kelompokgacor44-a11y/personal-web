About Norway High School

Norway High School is committed to providing excellence in education, fostering a nurturing environment where students can thrive academically, athletically, and personally. Our dedicated faculty and state-of-the-art facilities ensure that every student receives the support they need to succeed.

With a rich history of academic achievement and a vibrant community, we prepare our students for the challenges of tomorrow while celebrating the traditions that make our school unique.

Academic Excellence

Our comprehensive curriculum is designed to challenge and inspire students at every level. From advanced placement courses to specialized programs in STEM, arts, and humanities, we offer a diverse range of opportunities for academic growth.

Our experienced educators are passionate about teaching and dedicated to helping each student reach their full potential through personalized instruction and innovative teaching methods.

Athletics Program

Norway High School boasts a proud tradition of athletic excellence. Our comprehensive sports program includes basketball, soccer, track and field, swimming, and many other competitive sports that promote teamwork, discipline, and physical fitness.

Our state-of-the-art athletic facilities and experienced coaching staff provide students with the resources they need to excel both on and off the field.

Student Life

Life at Norway High School extends far beyond the classroom. Our vibrant student community offers numerous clubs, organizations, and activities that cater to diverse interests, from debate team and drama club to robotics and environmental initiatives.

We believe in developing well-rounded individuals who are prepared to make meaningful contributions to society. Our student life programs foster leadership, creativity, and social responsibility.

Alumni Network

Our alumni community spans the globe, with graduates making their mark in diverse fields including business, medicine, education, arts, and public service. The bonds formed at Norway High School last a lifetime.

We maintain strong connections with our alumni through regular events, mentorship programs, and networking opportunities that benefit current students and graduates alike.
